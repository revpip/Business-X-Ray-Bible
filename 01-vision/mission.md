# Mission & Vision

## Mission

Business X-Ray exists to help businesses understand themselves well enough to improve continuously.

## Public Promise

Every recommendation should be:

1. Explainable
2. Practical
3. Prioritised
4. Evidence-led
5. Designed to create measurable business value

## What We Are Building

Business X-Ray is not merely a WordPress plugin, consultancy service or AI tool.

It is intended to become a repeatable business diagnostic method, delivered through software, supported by evidence, and refined through real-world outcomes.

## North Star

> Help business owners make better decisions with greater confidence.

## Emotional Outcome

A business owner should leave the Business X-Ray experience feeling:

- understood
- clearer
- calmer
- more confident
- ready to act

## Product Outcome

Every Business X-Ray should identify:

- what is working
- what is causing friction
- what should be improved first
- why it matters
- what action to take next
- how progress will be measured
