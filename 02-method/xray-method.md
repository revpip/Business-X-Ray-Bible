# The X-Ray Method™

The X-Ray Method is the repeatable methodology behind every Business X-Ray.

## Stages

### 1. Discover
Understand the business, owner, goals, frustrations and context.

### 2. Observe
Collect structured evidence through assessments, conversations, website scans, documents and operational information.

### 3. Diagnose
Identify root causes, not surface symptoms.

### 4. Prioritise
Rank opportunities by likely value, difficulty, urgency and strategic importance.

### 5. Improve
Turn findings into a practical action plan.

### 6. Measure
Track whether recommendations were implemented and whether they produced meaningful change.

### 7. Repeat
Re-run the X-Ray periodically so the business keeps improving.

## The Four Questions

Every report must answer:

1. What did we find?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What should happen next?
4. How will we know it worked?

- Technology
- Growth
- Resilience

## Recommendation Rules

A recommendation should only be made when it is:

- relevant to the business context
- understandable to the owner
- realistically actionable
- prioritised clearly
- linked to a measurable outcome where possible
